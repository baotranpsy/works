Bao-Tran Nguyen-Duong

I received my Bachelor's degree in Psychology from Ho Chi Minh City University of Education, Vietnam. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree at the Graduate Institute of Mind, Brain and Consciousness (GIMBC), Taipei Medical University, Taiwan, where I was awarded a scholarship covering full tuition. I am affiliated with The Brain State Lab under the supervision of Associate Professor Niall Duncan, and I am involved in ongoing research projects. My research interests lie in neuroscience, with a particular focus on brain states and their underlying neural mechanisms. Prior to this, my academic work primarily centered on mental health.
